About Me!

Who am I?!

I love building things!—whether it’s a small script to automate a task, a full-stack app or a short, fun video. But what excites me even more is finding the most efficient and user-friendly way to do it.

I am currently seeking internships where I can learn, grow, and put my skills to work in a hands-on environment. I want to collaborate, solve real problems, and create effective solutions.

My Journey into Tech

My passion for technology began in 9th grade when I built my first computer. The hands-on experience of sourcing parts and solving challenges sparked my curiosity, while creating custom Minecraft mods deepened my love for coding and understanding how systems work.

Current Passions

Today, I’m passionate about machine learning, particularly in analyzing and classifying data, and I’m actively self-studying digital electronics and Verilog. I enjoy exploring the intersection of software and hardware, combining creativity with technical skills to develop impactful solutions.

I also have a strong interest in the business side of technology. Managing a 50,000-follower social media page during my Minecraft modding journey taught me the importance of understanding user needs, effective marketing, and creating products that truly resonate with people.

Roles I am seeking
